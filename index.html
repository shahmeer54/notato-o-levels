Mughal Empire — Quick Notes

Summary • Politics, Culture, Architecture

Founding

Babur (1526) defeated Ibrahim Lodi at Panipat and established the Mughal dynasty. Important rulers include Akbar, Shah Jahan and Aurangzeb.

Achievements

  • Architecture: Taj Mahal, Red Fort
  • Art: Mughal miniature painting
  • Administration: Mansabdari system
